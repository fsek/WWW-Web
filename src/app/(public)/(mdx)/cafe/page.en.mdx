Hilbert Café is the F-guilds own student driven café. Sandwiches, coffee and snacks are served everyday at a reasonable price. Ordinary opening hours are monday to friday every lecture-week 08:00 - 15:00. Opening hours may vary depending on worker availability. Since the café is dependent on voluntary work, we are in need of café workers. To work in the café is a fun experience and a fantastic way of meeting new people. To sign up for a work shift, use the F-app or use the link here below.

<a href="/cafe-shifts"><Button>Sign up to work here!</Button></a>

Institutions and guilds or other student organizations at LTH or LU are welcome to order sandwiches from us for lunch lectures or similar events. Please place your order a good time in advance if you are interested. If you have any questions, feel free to [contact the head of the café](mailto:cafe@fsektionen.se).
